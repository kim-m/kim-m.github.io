Welcome to Digital Detox!

This blog includes a collection of ideas to enjoy life in the moment, rather than through our screens. A reminder of what is important and perhaps how to break the tech addiction.

Many studies and statistics have come to the conclusion that excessive screen use can have a negative impact on a person. More younger individuals between the age range of 2-16 are constantly glued to a screen.

These articles provide suggestions that may help break the cycle. From spending time with the family, no gadgets at the dinner table or simply reading a good old paperback book.
