Are you a nomophobe?

Nomophobia:

The irrational fear of being without your mobile phone or being unable to use your phone for some reason, such as the absence of a signal or running out of minutes or battery power.

Did you know?

The average person checks a cell phone 110 times a day!

Phone Addiction Causes:

  • Anxiety
  • "Text Neck"
  • Obesity
  • Depression
  • Eye Fatigue
  • Headaches
  • Sleep Disturbances
  • Distractions / Car Accidents
  • Lack of Social Interaction
